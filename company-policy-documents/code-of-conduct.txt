Company Code of Conduct

1. Professional Behavior:
All employees must maintain professionalism in their conduct and communication within and outside the workplace.

2. Confidentiality:
Employees must ensure that any sensitive company information is kept confidential and is not shared with unauthorized individuals.

3. Harassment:
The company has a zero-tolerance policy for any form of harassment, including sexual harassment, verbal abuse, and bullying.
