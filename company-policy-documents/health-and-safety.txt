Health and Safety Policy

1. Workplace Safety:
The company is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. Employees are required to follow all safety protocols and report any potential hazards to the Safety Officer.

2. Emergency Procedures:
In the event of an emergency, employees must evacuate the building according to the posted evacuation routes. Regular fire drills will be conducted to ensure preparedness.

3. Ergonomics:
Employees are encouraged to maintain ergonomic practices at their workstations, including proper seating posture and regular breaks to reduce strain.

4. First Aid:
The company provides first-aid kits in all departments. In case of injury, employees should immediately report to the designated First Aid Officer for assistance.
