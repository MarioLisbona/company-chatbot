Company Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Unused leave can be carried forward to the next calendar year but must be used within 18 months.

2. Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave annually. A medical certificate is required for any sick leave exceeding two consecutive days.

3. Maternity and Paternity Leave:
Female employees are entitled to 12 weeks of maternity leave, while male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paternity leave.

4. Unpaid Leave:
Unpaid leave can be requested and is subject to management approval. A minimum of two weeks' notice is required.
