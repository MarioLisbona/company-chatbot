Work From Home Policy

1. Eligibility:
Employees who have completed at least six months of service are eligible to apply for work-from-home (WFH) arrangements. Approval is subject to management discretion.

2. Availability:
While working from home, employees are expected to be available during normal business hours and must communicate any changes in availability with their manager.

3. Equipment:
The company will provide necessary equipment for remote work, such as laptops and access to required software tools. Employees are responsible for ensuring they have a stable internet connection.

4. Security:
Employees must ensure that all company data accessed during WFH is protected. Use company-provided VPN for secure access to internal systems.
