Data Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection:
The company collects personal information from employees for operational purposes, including payroll processing, benefits management, and performance evaluations.

2. Data Storage:
All personal data is stored securely on company servers with restricted access. The data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

3. Data Sharing:
The company does not share personal employee data with third parties unless required by law or authorized by the employee.

4. Employee Rights:
Employees have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal data by contacting the HR department. Requests will be processed within 30 days.
